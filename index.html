Biography

Mehdi Alimohammadi is currently a Ph.D. candidate in control systems engineering in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad. He is also a researcher at the Cognitive and Computing Laboratory (CCL). His work focuses on bridging control system theory and evolutionary optimization and also solving combinatorial problems. His current engagements are implementing a PSO electronic circuit and also a new encoding for optimizing structure-agnostic problems.
